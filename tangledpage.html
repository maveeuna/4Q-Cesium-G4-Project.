Rapunzel is a girl born with magical, healing hair.

Mother Gothel has imprisoned her in a tower. On her birthday, she
hopes to exit the tower and witness the release of the floating
lanterns. When Flynn Rider, a robber, finds her tower one day,
they strike a deal: she returns the stolen item to him,
and he helps her escape.

Rapunzel discovers the truth about her royal
ancestry and abilities during their adventure
together. She escapes Gothel's grasp and
reconciles with her biological parents, the
king and queen with Flynn's assistance.
