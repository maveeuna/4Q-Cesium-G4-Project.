A good-hearted and lovely princess's stepmother,
the Evil Queen, is envious of her good looks. The Queen
orders Snow White's death so she can continue to be
the most beautiful of them all. Snow White,
however, manages to get away into the forest and
seeks refuge with the seven dwarfs.

The Queen disguises herself and deceives
Snow White into eating a poisoned apple,
which puts her into a deep sleep despite
her concealing. After a while, a prince
finds her, kisses her to break the
enchantment, and they lead a
happy life together.
